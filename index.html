Features
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built by a freelancer who knows the pain. Every feature solves a real problem from real experience on Freelancer.com.

Proposals in 30 seconds

Stop spending 15 minutes on each bid. MPOP generates a full tailored proposal in under 30 seconds so you can bid on more projects every day.

Reads the full description

MPOP reads the entire project — features, tech stack, goals — and references specific details in your proposal. Clients notice immediately.

Tone & length control

Professional, Friendly, Bold, or Concise tone. Short, Medium, or Detailed length. Each selection generates a completely different structured proposal.

Proposal history

Regenerate for a fresh version and switch between your last 3 proposals. Compare them and pick the strongest one before submitting.

Real-time char validation

Freelancer requires a minimum of 100 characters. MPOP warns you in real time so you never get blocked on submission — a small but critical detail.

Free AI — zero cost

Powered by Groq's free LLM API. You only pay for MPOP — the AI behind it costs you nothing. No hidden per-proposal fees. Ever.
